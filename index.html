The Hand Behind the Art

I believe that a simple line can convey more emotion than a thousand words. With over 10 years of experience in traditional media, I focus on the tactile relationship between graphite and paper.

My work explores the fragility of human memory and the structural beauty of the world around us. Currently based in London, accepting commissions worldwide.
